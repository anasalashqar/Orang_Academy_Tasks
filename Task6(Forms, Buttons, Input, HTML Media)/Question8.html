Fish

Description

Fish are fascinating and diverse creatures that inhabit various aquatic environments, from the deep, dark depths of the ocean to the tranquil waters of freshwater lakes and rivers. With over 30,000 species known to science, fish display an astounding range of shapes, sizes, and colors, each adapting to their specific environments and lifestyles. Many fish possess unique features that aid in their survival; for instance, some have developed bioluminescence to attract mates or lure prey, while others, like the pufferfish, can inflate themselves as a defense mechanism.
